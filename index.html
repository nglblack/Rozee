About RoZee

RoZee is led by Rose Fischer (Rozee), a dynamic vocalist bringing versatile live performances to Southern Utah. With a repertoire spanning country, rock, pop, soul, and acoustic styles, Rose creates memorable experiences perfectly suited to your event.

Whether you need intimate acoustic sets or full-band energy, RoZee delivers professional entertainment with a personal touch. The band configuration adapts to your venue size, budget, and musical preferences.

From weddings and corporate events to venue performances and private parties, Rose and her talented supporting musicians bring professionalism, flexibility, and passion to every show.

Lead Vocals

Rozee is the heart and soul of the band, bringing powerhouse vocals and magnetic stage presence to every performance. With roots in soul and R&B, her voice is impossible to forget.
